Biography

Alan is currently pursuing a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science at the Erik Jonsson School of Engineering & Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas. He is currently in his second year at the Department of Computer Science and is being mentored by Ashkan Yousefpour in research related to Fog Computing.

His research involves exploring the applications of resilient distributed deep neural networks in the Fog. His other interests include machine learning, Internet of Things, and virtual reality.
