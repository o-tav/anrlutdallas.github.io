Biography

Riti is a Ph.D. candidate in Telecommunications Engineering at University of Texas at Dallas, USA. Prior to joining ANRL as a Ph.D. student, she did her Masters in Telecommunications at UT Dallas. Her research investigates the theoretical and practical challenges to improve the survivability and reliability of the optical networks during correlated failure events like disasters.
