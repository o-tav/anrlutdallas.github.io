Biography

Genya is a Ph.D. student at the Department of Computer Science at the University of Texas at Dallas. His interests are survivability of layered or interdependent networks, Software Defined Networking (SDN), and graph optimization. He obtained his master's degree in March 2016 from Soka University, Japan.

Currently, he has been funded by the Japanese goverment for his degree research.
