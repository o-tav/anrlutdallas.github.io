Biography

Jian Kong received his B.Eng. degree in automation from Shandong University in 2007, and the M.S. degree in control theory and engineering from Beihang University in 2010. He is currently a third year Ph.D. student in telecommunication engineering, University of Texas at Dallas. His research interests are network virtualization, software-defined networking, optical networking designing and modeling, and cloud computing.
